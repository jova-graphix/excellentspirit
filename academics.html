Ages 2 – 5

Nursery & Kindergarten

A joyful introduction to learning through play, stories, and creative activities. Our early years program builds strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills in a warm, nurturing environment.

We understand that early childhood is a critical period for development. Our trained teachers create engaging activities that stimulate curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning.

Program Highlights:

✓ Play-based learning
✓ Early literacy & numeracy
✓ Social skills development
✓ Creative arts & music
✓ Storytelling sessions
✓ Outdoor play activities
Ages 6 – 12

Primary School

A comprehensive primary education following the Ghana Education Service curriculum. Our primary program develops strong academic skills while encouraging students to explore their interests and talents.

Our dedicated teachers use modern teaching methods to make learning engaging and effective. Students develop critical thinking skills, build strong foundations in core subjects, and grow as confident learners.

Subjects Offered:

✓ English Language
✓ Mathematics
✓ Science
✓ Social Studies
✓ ICT
✓ French
✓ Religious & Moral Education
✓ Creative Arts

Ages 12 – 15

Junior High School (JHS)

Rigorous preparation for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) and beyond. Our JHS program equips students with the knowledge and skills needed for success in senior high school.

We provide focused academic support, career guidance, and leadership development opportunities. Our goal is to ensure every student is confident and prepared for the next stage of their education journey.

Program Features:

✓ BECE preparation
✓ Core subjects mastery
✓ Career guidance
✓ Leadership development
✓ Exam preparation support
✓ Mentorship programs
