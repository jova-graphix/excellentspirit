Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions from parents.

We accept children from ages 2 to 15, covering Nursery/Kindergarten (ages 2-5), Primary School (ages 6-12), and Junior High School/JHS (ages 12-15).

We follow the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum while incorporating modern teaching methods to ensure comprehensive education.

Our school hours are [School Hours] Monday through Friday. Please contact us for specific class schedules.

The enrollment process is simple: visit our school, get the admission form, submit required documents, and complete registration. Visit our Admissions page for details.

Please contact our school office directly to inquire about transportation options and availability in your area.

We offer various activities including sports and games, cultural activities, music and drama, arts and crafts, clubs and societies, and educational field trips.

We believe in strong parent-teacher partnerships. Parents are welcome to attend school events, parent-teacher meetings, and volunteer for activities. We also maintain regular communication about your child's progress.
