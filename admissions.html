Admissions

We welcome children from all backgrounds to join our nurturing learning community. Apply today!

How to Apply

Simple 4-Step Admission Process

Our admissions process is straightforward and designed to make enrollment easy for families.

1

Visit Our School

Schedule a visit to tour our facilities, meet our teachers, and learn more about our programs.

2

Get Admission Form

Collect the admission form from our school office or request one via email or WhatsApp.

3

Submit Documents

Complete the form and submit with required documents including birth certificate and photos.

4

Welcome Onboard!

Once approved, complete the enrollment process and welcome to the Excellent Spirit family!

Documentation

Required Documents

To complete your child's admission, please prepare the following documents. If you have any questions about the requirements, feel free to contact us.

  • ✓

    Birth Certificate

    Original and photocopy for verification

  • ✓

    Passport-Size Photographs

    4 recent passport-size photos of the child

  • ✓

    Previous School Report Card

    If transferring from another school (if applicable)

  • ✓

    Immunization / Health Record

    Up-to-date vaccination records

  • ✓

    Parent/Guardian ID

    National ID card or passport of parent/guardian
