About Our School

Building Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Excellent Spirit International School is a trusted educational institution located in Ashaley Botwe, Old Town, Accra, Ghana. We are committed to providing quality education that nurtures the whole child — academically, morally, and socially.

Our school offers a supportive learning environment where children are encouraged to discover their potential, develop strong values, and excel in all areas of life.

Our Programs

Academic Programs We Offer

Comprehensive education from nursery through junior high school, following the Ghana Education Service curriculum.

👶

Nursery & Kindergarten

Ages 2 – 5

A joyful introduction to learning through play, stories, and creative activities. We build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills.

  • ✓ Play-based learning
  • ✓ Early literacy & numeracy
  • ✓ Social skills development
  • ✓ Creative arts & music
📖

Primary School

Ages 6 – 12

A comprehensive primary education following the Ghana Education Service curriculum. Students develop strong academic skills while exploring interests.

  • ✓ GES curriculum
  • ✓ English & Mathematics
  • ✓ Science & Social Studies
  • ✓ ICT & French
🎓

Junior High School

Ages 12 – 15

Rigorous preparation for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) and beyond. We equip students for success in senior high school.

  • ✓ BECE preparation
  • ✓ Core subjects mastery
  • ✓ Career guidance
  • ✓ Leadership development
