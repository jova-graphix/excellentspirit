Follow the steps below to get started

This page edits the text and photos shown across your website, syncing instantly to every visitor on every device. To turn it on, connect a free Firebase project — it takes about 5 minutes.

  1. 1

    Go to console.firebase.google.com and create a free project.

  2. 2

    Enable Firestore Database and Authentication (Email/Password) from the Build menu.

  3. 3

    In Authentication → Users, add one user with your email and a password.

  4. 4

    Register a Web app in Project Settings, copy the firebaseConfig values into CMS_FIREBASE_CONFIG in every page file, and apply the security rules from FIREBASE-SETUP.md.

  5. 5

    Re-upload the updated files to your host, then refresh this page.

Until then, every page shows its built-in content — nothing is broken.
